Matthew Suh

Information Science Major (UX/HCI)

Hello! My name is Matthew Suh and I am a student in the College of Arts & Sciences at Cornell University. I am currently a sophomore studying Information Science, with a concentration in UX/HCI. I also participate in UX/HCI research in the Communication and Collaborative Technologies Lab as an Undergraduate Research Assistant. My project involves research with telepresence robots!

A little about me:

I grew up in Potomac, Maryland, just a thirty minute drive to the nation's capitol, Washington DC. There, I attended Winston Churchill High School and participated in a multitude of activities. I was President of the Robotics Team, Vice President of the Debate Team, where I travelled to Harvard and Princeton to compete in both national and international debate tournaments

On campus, I spend the most of my time studying, of course, but in my free time I help my church plan and organize fellowship events and activities. I also enjoy playing ultimate frisbee casually and making CSS art on codepen.io. I am an avid football fan of the Washington Redskins and Green Bay Packers, and hockey fan of both the Washington Capitals and of course Cornell Big Red.
