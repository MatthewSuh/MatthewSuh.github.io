About Me

Hello! I’m Matthew Suh, a senior at Cornell University studying UX Design.

I love solving problems for people before they realize that they have a problem.


How I got into UX

I began my college career as a Biology major intent on going to dental school. That all changed when I took my first CS course and attended my first hackathon.

At the hackathon, my friend and I designed a website with the purpose of helping people relax during exams by using calming colors and images. The entire experience of building a website with a purpose, as well as teaching ourselves HTML and CSS from the ground up, inspired me to drop Biology and switch to UX.

From there, I've learned about different UX methods, helped improve the usability of the Cornell University Libraries, assisted in research as a part of the Communication and Collaborative Technologies Lab at Cornell, interned at Metrik, and made this portfolio website.

I'm looking for opportunities to both apply what I know now, as well as expand on this knowledge and experience in ways that can also help other people.
